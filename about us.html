About HYGENEE.LTD

HYGENEE.LTD is a community-focused initiative based in Mushishiro Sector, Muhanga District, Rwanda. We are committed to improving public health and environmental cleanliness by empowering residents to report waste problems quickly and easily. Our mission is to promote responsible waste disposal and connect citizens with local waste management teams for a cleaner, healthier neighborhood.
